The Challenge in Traditional Clinic Management

Panchakarma centers face unique operational hurdles that generic software can't solve, leading to inefficiencies and compromised patient care.

Inefficient Manual Scheduling

Manual scheduling of complex therapy sessions is inefficient and prone to human errors, leading to booking conflicts and wasted time.

Unreliable Paper Records

Paper-based records are susceptible to loss or damage, making them unreliable and difficult to manage and access for historical data.

Limited Digital Management

Poor patient follow-up and lack of real-time progress tracking lead to inconsistent therapy quality and compromised patient care.

Poor Patient Experience

Fragmented communication and a lack of personalized notifications can lead to a poor overall experience for patients.

OUR SOLUTION

Integrating Authenticity with Modern Efficiency

AyurSutra addresses these challenges by providing a comprehensive digital solution. Our platform automates complex therapy scheduling, ensures patients are well-informed with timely notifications, and empowers practitioners with tools to monitor progress in real-time, delivering a truly patient-centric experience.

  • Automate Scheduling: Effortlessly plan, modify, and view therapy sessions for both patients and practitioners.
  • Ensure Compliance: Automated notifications for pre- and post-procedure precautions via in-app, SMS, and email.
  • Enhance Patient-Centric Care: Provide real-time tracking and a feedback loop to refine treatments for better outcomes.

About AyurSutra

AyurSutra is a solution developed to address the unique challenges faced by modern Ayurvedic clinics. Panchakarma therapy requires meticulous record-keeping, precise scheduling, and continuous patient progress tracking. Traditional methods, often involving paper records and manual scheduling, are inefficient and prone to errors.

Our software provides a comprehensive digital platform that automates these processes, allowing practitioners to focus on patient care. By centralizing data and providing intelligent tools, AyurSutra aims to enhance the efficiency, accuracy, and overall quality of Ayurvedic healthcare delivery.

This project is our entry for the Smart India Hackathon, and we are committed to building a robust and impactful solution for the healthcare sector.
